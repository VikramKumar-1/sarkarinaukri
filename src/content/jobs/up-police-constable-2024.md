### UP Police Constable Notification 2024 : Age Limit as on 01/07/2023

* Minimum Age : 18 Years.
* Maximum Age : 25 Years for Male.
* Maximum Age : 28 Years for Female.
* Age Relaxation Extra as per UPPBPB Rules.

### UP Police Constable Eligibility

* 10+2 Intermediate Exam Passed in Any Recognized Board in India.
* Read the Notification for More Details.
